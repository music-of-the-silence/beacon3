(About Us)

Beacon Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. provides small molecule manufacturing services with a strong focus on continuous improvement and cost control while maintaining quality.

Beacon is fully supported by Bio-Pharma Services India for all quality, engineering, tech transfer, EH&S, procurement, sales and management related matters. All procedures used at Bio-Pharma India were developed to meet Beacon Services standards, and comply with local regulations. The manufacturing facilities are IN FDA inspected, and we expect additional international regulatory inspections in the coming months and years.

The Beacon Pharmaceuticals site is located in the Special Economic Zone, Ramky Pharma City, Vizakhapatnam, Kolkata, India. The whole complex is devoted to pharmaceuticals manufacturing with centralized services.
